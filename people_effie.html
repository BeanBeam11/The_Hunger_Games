Effie Trinket


"We're a team, aren't we? And I'm so proud of my victors. You both deserved so much better, I'm truly sorry..."


- Effie Trinket


Effie Trinket is the escort of the District 12 tributes, including Katniss Everdeen and Peeta Mellark, being an advisor to the pair. By the end of Mockingjay, she is the only Escort from the 74th and 75th Hunger Games alive after the Second Rebellion.
